FIFA World Cup 2014 News

The biggest occasion in the history of the FIFA World Cup qualifier – and indeed in the history of international football – was recorded on 11 April 2001, when Australia beat American Samoa 31–0.

This legendary match also brought global stardom for Archie Thompson, whose 13-goal haul set a new world record, which stands to this day, for an individual player in a single international match.
